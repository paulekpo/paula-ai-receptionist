08 / STRAIGHT ANSWERS

Before Paula answers your first call.

Still deciding? These are the concerns that matter most in a real implementation.

Will an AI voice hurt our personal reputation?

It should not be deployed carelessly. Paula is configured around your brand tone, business facts, escalation rules, and human handoff. Many businesses use her only for overflow and after-hours coverage so the human team remains the main experience.

Do I have to replace my receptionist?

No. Paula can serve strictly as backup for missed, busy, and after-hours calls.

What happens when someone misses us?

The workflow can send an immediate SMS, capture name and need, assess urgency, confirm the best number, create a CRM opportunity, and notify the right person.

Is the revenue calculator a guarantee?

No. It is adjustable scenario math. Actual recovered revenue depends on call quality, service demand, pricing, team follow-up, and close rate.

What costs are not included in $299/month?

Your GoHighLevel account and metered phone, SMS, and AI usage are separate. Cost varies by volume, carrier, country, model, and configuration.

What can be changed after launch?

Request updates to routing, opening hours, prompts, FAQs, services, qualification, notifications, CRM steps, and follow-up sequences. Extra consultation is available at $35–50/hour.
